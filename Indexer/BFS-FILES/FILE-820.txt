      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="The_Moon">
        <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">
         The Moon
        </a>
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_extraterrestrial_memorials&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: The Moon">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <i>
         <a href="/wiki/Fallen_Astronaut" title="Fallen Astronaut">
          Fallen Astronaut
         </a>
        </i>
        , is a lunar plaque for lives lost in space exploration.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8">
         <a href="#cite_note-8">
          [8]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </li>
       <li>
        Apollo 11, 12, 14, 15, 16, and 17 each left a
        <a href="/wiki/Lunar_plaque" title="Lunar plaque">
         plaque
        </a>
        and
        <a href="/wiki/Lunar_Flag_Assembly" title="Lunar Flag Assembly">
         flag
        </a>
        at their landing sites.
       </li>
       <li>
        Pennants of the Soviet Union on
        <a href="/wiki/Luna_2" title="Luna 2">
         Luna 2
        </a>
        impactor
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9">
         <a href="#cite_note-9">
          [9]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="/wiki/Flag_of_India" title="Flag of India">
         Flag of India
        </a>
        on
        <a href="/wiki/Indian_Space_Research_Organisation" title="Indian Space Research Organisation">
         ISRO
        </a>
        's
        <a href="/wiki/Moon_Impact_Probe" title="Moon Impact Probe">
         Moon Impact Probe
        </a>
        that was released by
        <a href="/wiki/Chandrayaan-1" title="Chandrayaan-1">
         Chandrayaan-1
        </a>
        and impacted the Lunar surface on 14 November 2008.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TOI_10-0">
         <a href="#cite_note-TOI-10">
          [10]
         </a>
        </sup>
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11">
         <a href="#cite_note-11">
          [11]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </li>
      </ul>
