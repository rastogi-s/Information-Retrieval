      <p>
       <b>
        George Rigaud
       </b>
       (11 August 1905 – 17 January 1984) was an Argentine film actor. He was born in
       <a href="/wiki/Buenos_Aires" title="Buenos Aires">
        Buenos Aires
       </a>
       , Argentina, and appeared in 194 films between 1932 and 1981. He was killed in a road accident in
       <a href="/wiki/Madrid" title="Madrid">
        Madrid
       </a>
       , Spain.
      </p>
