      <p>
       <b>
        FK Comae Berenices
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Variable_star" title="Variable star">
        variable star
       </a>
       that varies in
       <a href="/wiki/Apparent_magnitude" title="Apparent magnitude">
        apparent magnitude
       </a>
       between 8.14m and 8.33m over a period of 2.4 days. It is the prototype for the
       <a href="/wiki/Variable_star#FK_Comae_Berenices_variables" title="Variable star">
        FK Com
       </a>
       class of variable stars. The variability of FK Com stars may be caused by large, cool spots on the rotating surfaces of the stars.
      </p>
