      <p>
       <b>
        Angelina Pagano
       </b>
       (1888–1962) was an Argentine
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stage_actress" title="Stage actress">
        stage
       </a>
       and
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Film_actress" title="Film actress">
        film actress
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       Pagano appeared in eighteen films, but was best known for her stage acting and work as a
       <a href="/wiki/Theatre_director" title="Theatre director">
        theatre director
       </a>
       .
      </p>
