      <p>
       The
       <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">
        Moon's
       </a>
       surface is covered in many interesting features. Among these are several large
       <a href="/wiki/Valley" title="Valley">
        valleys
       </a>
       that have been given names. These are listed below.
      </p>
      <p>
       Most of these valleys are named after a nearby
       <a href="/wiki/Impact_crater" title="Impact crater">
        crater
       </a>
       ; see the
       <a href="/wiki/List_of_craters_on_the_Moon" title="List of craters on the Moon">
        list of craters on the Moon
       </a>
       for more information.
      </p>
