      <p>
       In
       <a href="/wiki/Astronomy" title="Astronomy">
        astronomy
       </a>
       , a tetrad is a set of four total
       <a href="/wiki/Lunar_eclipse" title="Lunar eclipse">
        lunar eclipses
       </a>
       within two years.

        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
