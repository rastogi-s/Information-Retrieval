event on the exact day that it is displayed and images are sometimes repeated 2 however the pictures and descriptions often relate to current events in astronomy and space exploration the text has several hyperlinks to more pictures and websites for more information the images are either visible spectrum photographs images taken at non-visible wavelengths and displayed in false color video
