      <p>
       The
       <b>
        Whitbread Awards
       </b>
       (1971–2005), called
       <b>
        <a href="/wiki/Costa_Book_Awards" title="Costa Book Awards">
         Costa Book Awards
        </a>
       </b>
       since 2006, are literary awards in the United Kingdom, awarded both for high literary merit but also for works considered enjoyable reading. This page gives details of the awards given in the year
       <b>
        <a href="/wiki/1997_in_literature" title="1997 in literature">
         1997
        </a>
       </b>
       .
      </p>
