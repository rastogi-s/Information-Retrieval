      <p>
       <b>
        Ocnele Mari
       </b>
       is a town located in
       <a href="/wiki/V%C3%A2lcea_County" title="Vâlcea County">
        Vâlcea County
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">
        Romania
       </a>
       . The town administers eight villages: Buda, Cosota, Făcăi, Gura Suhașului, Lunca, Ocnița, Slătioarele and Țeica.
      </p>
