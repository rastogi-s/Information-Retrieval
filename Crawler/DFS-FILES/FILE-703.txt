      <p>
       <b>
        Caldas
       </b>
       (
       <small>
        Spanish pronunciation:
       </small>
       <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)">
        <a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Spanish" title="Help:IPA/Spanish">
         [ˈkaldas]
        </a>
       </span>
       ) is a
       <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_Colombia" title="Departments of Colombia">
        department
       </a>
       of
       <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">
        Colombia
       </a>
       named after Colombian patriotic figure
       <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Jos%C3%A9_de_Caldas" title="Francisco José de Caldas">
        Francisco José de Caldas
       </a>
       . It is part of the
       <a href="/wiki/Paisa_Region" title="Paisa Region">
        Paisa Region
       </a>
       and its capital is
       <a href="/wiki/Manizales" title="Manizales">
        Manizales
       </a>
       . The population of Caldas is 1,030,062, and its area is 7,291 km². Caldas is also part of the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Colombian_Coffee-Growers_Axis" title="Colombian Coffee-Growers Axis">
        Colombian Coffee-Growers Axis
       </a>
       region along with the
       <a href="/wiki/Risaralda_Department" title="Risaralda Department">
        Risaralda
       </a>
       and
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Quindio_Department" title="Quindio Department">
        Quindio
       </a>
       departments.
      </p>

      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Demography">
        Demography
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Caldas_Department&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Demography">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       The population of Caldas is 984,128 (2013), half of whom live in Manizales.. The racial composition is:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        White / Mestizo (93.16%)
       </li>
       <li>
        Amerindian or Indigenous (4.29%)
       </li>
       <li>
        Black or Afro-Colombian (2.54%)
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       The local inhabitants of Caldas are known as caldenses. Of the five main regional groups in Colombia, the predominant group in Caldas are known as paisa, referring to those living in the Paisa region, which covers most of Antioquia, as well as the departments of Caldas, Risaralda and Quindío.
      </p>
