   <h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">
    List of Spanish words of Germanic origin
   </h1>
   <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
    <div class="noprint" id="siteSub">
     From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
    </div>
    <div id="contentSub">
    </div>
    <div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
     Jump to:
     <a href="#mw-head">
      navigation
     </a>
     ,
     <a href="#p-search">
      search
     </a>
    </div>
    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <div class="mw-parser-output">
      <p>
       This is a list of some
       <b>
        <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">
         Spanish
        </a>
        words of
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Germanic_(language)" title="Germanic (language)">
         Germanic origin
        </a>
       </b>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       The list includes words from
       <a href="/wiki/Gothic_language" title="Gothic language">
        Visigothic
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Frankish_language" title="Frankish language">
        Frankish
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Lombards" title="Lombards">
        Langobardic
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Middle_Dutch" title="Middle Dutch">
        Middle Dutch
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Middle_High_German" title="Middle High German">
        Middle High German
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Middle_Low_German" title="Middle Low German">
        Middle Low German
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_English_language" title="Old English language">
        Old English
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Old_High_German" title="Old High German">
        Old High German
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_Norse_language" title="Old Norse language">
        Old Norse
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">
        Old Swedish
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">
        English
       </a>
       , and finally, words which come from
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Germanic_(language)" title="Germanic (language)">
        Germanic
       </a>
       with the specific source unknown.
      </p>
      <p>
       Some of these words existed in
       <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">
        Latin
       </a>
       as
       <a href="/wiki/Loanword" title="Loanword">
        loanwords
       </a>
       from other
       <a href="/wiki/Language" title="Language">
        languages
       </a>
       . Some of these words have alternate
       <a href="/wiki/Etymology" title="Etymology">
        etymologies
       </a>
       and may also appear on a list of
       <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">
        Spanish
       </a>
       words from a different
       <a href="/wiki/Language" title="Language">
        language
       </a>
       . Some words contain non-Germanic elements (see
       <i>
        béisbol
       </i>
       in the Middle English section). Any form with an
       <a href="/wiki/Asterisk" title="Asterisk">
        asterisk
       </a>
       (*) is unattested and therefore
       <a href="/wiki/Hypothesis" title="Hypothesis">
        hypothetical
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
