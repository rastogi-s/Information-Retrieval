      <p>
       The
       <b>
        Castilian languages
       </b>
       are
       <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">
        Castilian (Spanish)
       </a>
       and its closest relatives. Besides derivatives of Spanish such as
       <a href="/wiki/Judaeo-Spanish" title="Judaeo-Spanish">
        Ladino
       </a>
       , this refers principally to
       <a href="/wiki/Extremaduran_language" title="Extremaduran language">
        Extremaduran
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Name">
        Name
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>

         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       The term "
       <i>
        Castilian languages
       </i>
       " is used in the 17th edition of
       <i>
        Ethnologue
       </i>
       . This grouping is used virtually nowhere else.
      </p>
