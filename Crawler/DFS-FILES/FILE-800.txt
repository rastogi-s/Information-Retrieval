   <h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">
    Longest word in Spanish
   </h1>
   <div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
    <div class="noprint" id="siteSub">
     From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
    </div>
    <div id="contentSub">
    </div>
    <div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">
     Jump to:
     <a href="#mw-head">
      navigation
     </a>
     ,
     <a href="#p-search">
      search
     </a>
    </div>
    <div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en">
     <div class="mw-parser-output">
      <p>
       This article describes some of the
       <a href="/wiki/Longest_words" title="Longest words">
        longest words
       </a>
       in the
       <a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">
        Spanish language
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       <i>
        <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/esternocleidooccipitomastoideos" title="wikt:esternocleidooccipitomastoideos">
         Esternocleidooccipitomastoideos
        </a>
       </i>
       (31-letters) is the plural of the noun
       <i>
        <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/esternocleidooccipitomastoideo" title="wikt:esternocleidooccipitomastoideo">
         esternocleidooccipitomastoideo
        </a>
       </i>
       , which is the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sternocleidomastoid" title="Sternocleidomastoid">
        sternocleidomastoid
       </a>
       , a muscle in the human neck.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Calzado_1-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-Calzado-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       The word has a 22-letter
       <a href="/wiki/Synonym" title="Synonym">
        synonym
       </a>
       :
       <i>
        <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/esternocleidomastoideo" title="wikt:esternocleidomastoideo">
         esternocleidomastoideo
        </a>
       </i>
       ,
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RAE_esterno_2-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-RAE_esterno-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Erichsen_3-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-Erichsen-3">
         [3]
        </a>
       </sup>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Peninou_Tixa_4-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-Peninou_Tixa-4">
         [4]
        </a>
       </sup>
       which is shorter because it omits the Latin prefix
       <i>
        occipito-
       </i>
       (
       <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/occipital" title="wikt:occipital">
        occipital
       </a>
       ).
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">
        <a href="#cite_note-5">
         [5]
        </a>
       </sup>
       Both words are abbreviated as
       <i>
        ECOM
       </i>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Peninou_Tixa_4-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-Peninou_Tixa-4">
         [4]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       The 24-letter word
       <i>
        electroencefalografistas
       </i>
       , plural of
       <i>
        <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/es:electroencefalografista" title="wikt:es:electroencefalografista">
         electroencefalografista
        </a>
       </i>
       , means "electroencephalographists" or "
       <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/electroencephalographers" title="wikt:electroencephalographers">
        electroencephalographers
       </a>
       ": specialists in the brain measurement technology of
       <a href="/wiki/Electroencephalography" title="Electroencephalography">
        electroencephalography
       </a>
       (EEG).
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Erichsen_3-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-Erichsen-3">
         [3]
        </a>
       </sup>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-electroencefalografista_6-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-electroencefalografista-6">
         [6]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       The 23-letter adverb
       <i>
        <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/anticonstitucionalmente#Spanish" title="wikt:anticonstitucionalmente">
         anticonstitucionalmente
        </a>
       </i>
       means "
       <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/anticonstitutionally#English" title="wikt:anticonstitutionally">
        anticonstitutionally
       </a>
       ".
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Sundem_7-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-Sundem-7">
         [7]
        </a>
       </sup>
       <i>
        <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/anticonstitucionalmente#Portuguese" title="wikt:anticonstitucionalmente">
         Anticonstitucionalmente
        </a>
       </i>
       is also the
       <a href="/wiki/Portuguese_language" title="Portuguese language">
        Portuguese
       </a>
       translation; the French translation,
       <i>
        <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/fr:anticonstitutionnellement" title="wikt:fr:anticonstitutionnellement">
         anticonstitutionnellement
        </a>
       </i>
       , is an exceptionally long word as well (25 letters).
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Sundem_7-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-Sundem-7">
         [7]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Long_words">
        Long words
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Longest_word_in_Spanish&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Long words">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       In the table below, all of the Spanish nouns except for
       <i>
        arteriosclerosis
       </i>
       can be pluralised by adding an
       <i>
        s
       </i>
       to the end.
      </p>
      <p>
       The
       <i>
        RAE
       </i>
       column indicates whether the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Real_Academia_Espa%C3%B1ola" title="Real Academia Española">
        Real Academia Española
       </a>
       lists and defines the word in the
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/Diccionario_de_la_lengua_espa%C3%B1ola" title="Diccionario de la lengua española">
         Diccionario de la lengua española
        </a>
       </i>
       , its official dictionary.
      </p>
