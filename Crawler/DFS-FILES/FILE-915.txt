      <p>
       <b>
        Judaeo-Catalan
       </b>
       (
       <a href="/wiki/Hebrew_language" title="Hebrew language">
        Hebrew
       </a>
       :
       <span class="script-hebrew" dir="rtl" style="font-size: 115%; font-family: 'Ezra SIL', 'Ezra SIL SR', 'Keter Aram Tsova', 'Taamey Ashkenaz', 'Taamey David CLM', 'Taamey Frank CLM', 'Frenk Ruehl CLM', 'Keter YG', 'Shofar', 'David CLM', 'Hadasim CLM', 'Simple CLM', 'Nachlieli', 'SBL BibLit', 'SBL Hebrew', Cardo, Alef, 'Noto Serif Hebrew', 'Noto Sans Hebrew', 'David Libre', David, 'Times New Roman', Gisha, Arial, FreeSerif, FreeSans;">
        קטלאנית יהודית
       </span>
       ‬;
       <a href="/wiki/Catalan_language" title="Catalan language">
        Catalan
       </a>
       :
       <i>
        <span lang="ca" xml:lang="ca">
         judeocatalà
        </span>
       </i>
       ,
       <small>
        IPA:
       </small>
       <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)">
        <a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Catalan" title="Help:IPA/Catalan">
         [ʒuˌðewkətəˈɫa]
        </a>
       </span>
       ), also called
       <b>
        Catalanic
       </b>
       or
       <b>
        Qatalanit
       </b>
       (
       <a href="/wiki/Hebrew_language" title="Hebrew language">
        Hebrew
       </a>
       :
       <span class="script-hebrew" dir="rtl" style="font-size: 115%; font-family: 'Ezra SIL', 'Ezra SIL SR', 'Keter Aram Tsova', 'Taamey Ashkenaz', 'Taamey David CLM', 'Taamey Frank CLM', 'Frenk Ruehl CLM', 'Keter YG', 'Shofar', 'David CLM', 'Hadasim CLM', 'Simple CLM', 'Nachlieli', 'SBL BibLit', 'SBL Hebrew', Cardo, Alef, 'Noto Serif Hebrew', 'Noto Sans Hebrew', 'David Libre', David, 'Times New Roman', Gisha, Arial, FreeSerif, FreeSans;">
        קאטאלנית
       </span>
       ‬;
       <a href="/wiki/Catalan_language" title="Catalan language">
        Catalan
       </a>
       :
       <i>
        <span lang="ca" xml:lang="ca">
         catalànic
        </span>
       </i>
       or
       <i>
        <span lang="ca" xml:lang="ca">
         qatalanit
        </span>
       </i>
       ), was a
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jewish_language" title="Jewish language">
        Jewish language
       </a>
       spoken by the Jews in Northeastern
       <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">
        Spain
       </a>
       , especially in
       <a href="/wiki/Catalonia" title="Catalonia">
        Catalonia
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Valencia" title="Valencia">
        Valencia
       </a>
       and the
       <a href="/wiki/Balearic_Islands" title="Balearic Islands">
        Balearic Islands
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Linguistically, it shared many features in common with early
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Judaeo-Proven%C3%A7al" title="Judaeo-Provençal">
        Judaeo-Provençal
       </a>
       . However, historically, ethnically, and politically, speakers of Judaeo-Catalan were long distinct from Judaeo-Provençal, mostly as a result of the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Moorish" title="Moorish">
        Moorish
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Al-Andalus" title="Al-Andalus">
        conquest of Iberia
       </a>
       . The golden age of Judaeo-Catalan was between the early 12th century and 1492, when the Jews were expelled from Spain by
       <a href="/wiki/Alhambra_Decree" title="Alhambra Decree">
        Alhambra Decree
       </a>
       .
      </p>
