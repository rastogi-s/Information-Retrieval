      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         Argentina
        </b>
       </i>
       is an album by
       <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">
        New Zealand
       </a>
       trio
       <a href="/wiki/Thela" title="Thela">
        Thela
       </a>
       . It was recorded in
       <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">
        New York City
       </a>
       in March 1996, and produced by Thela with
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Tom_Surgal&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Tom Surgal (page does not exist)">
        Tom Surgal
       </a>
       . It was released in 1996 on
       <a href="/wiki/Thurston_Moore" title="Thurston Moore">
        Thurston Moore
       </a>
       's
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ecstatic_Peace" title="Ecstatic Peace">
        Ecstatic Peace
       </a>
       label. It consists of 6 untitled tracks.
      </p>
