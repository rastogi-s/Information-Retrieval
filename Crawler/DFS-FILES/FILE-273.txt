      <p>
       The
       <b>
        55th parallel north
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_latitude" title="Circle of latitude">
        circle of latitude
       </a>
       that is 55
       <a href="/wiki/Degree_(angle)" title="Degree (angle)">
        degrees
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/True_north" title="True north">
        north
       </a>
       of the
       <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">
        Earth's
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">
        equatorial plane
       </a>
       . It crosses
       <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">
        Europe
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">
        Asia
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">
        Pacific Ocean
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">
        North America
       </a>
       , and the
       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">
        Atlantic Ocean
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       At this latitude the
       <a href="/wiki/Sun" title="Sun">
        sun
       </a>
       is visible for 17 hours, 22 minutes during the
       <a href="/wiki/Summer_solstice" title="Summer solstice">
        summer solstice
       </a>
       and 7 hours, 10 minutes during the
       <a href="/wiki/Winter_solstice" title="Winter solstice">
        winter solstice
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>

      <h2>
       <span id="Notable_cities_and_towns_on_55.C2.B0N">
       </span>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Notable_cities_and_towns_on_55°N">
        Notable cities and towns on 55°N
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=55th_parallel_north&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Notable cities and towns on 55°N">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Starting at the Prime Meridian and heading eastwards:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <a href="/wiki/Omsk" title="Omsk">
         Omsk
        </a>
        , Omsk Oblast, Russia
       </li>

        </a>

       </li>

        <a href="/wiki/South_Shields" title="South Shields">
         South Shields
        </a>
        , England, UK
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Use_as_a_boundary">
        Use as a boundary
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=55th_parallel_north&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Use as a boundary">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       The 55th parallel serves as the southern boundary of
       <a href="/wiki/Nunavik" title="Nunavik">
        Nunavik
       </a>
       territory in
       <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">
        Quebec
       </a>
       .
      </p>
