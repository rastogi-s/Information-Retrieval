      <p>
       The
       <b>
        10th parallel north
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_latitude" title="Circle of latitude">
        circle of latitude
       </a>
       that is 10
       <a href="/wiki/Degree_(angle)" title="Degree (angle)">
        degrees
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/True_north" title="True north">
        north
       </a>
       of the
       <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">
        Earth's
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">
        equatorial plane
       </a>
       . It crosses
       <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">
        Africa
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">
        Indian Ocean
       </a>
       , the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indian_Subcontinent" title="Indian Subcontinent">
        Indian Subcontinent
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Southeast_Asia" title="Southeast Asia">
        Southeast Asia
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">
        Pacific Ocean
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Central_America" title="Central America">
        Central America
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">
        South America
       </a>
       and the
       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">
        Atlantic Ocean
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       At this latitude the
       <a href="/wiki/Sun" title="Sun">
        sun
       </a>
       is visible for 12 hours, 43 minutes during the
       <a href="/wiki/Summer_solstice" title="Summer solstice">
        summer solstice
       </a>
       and 11 hours, 33 minutes during the
       <a href="/wiki/Winter_solstice" title="Winter solstice">
        winter solstice
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       A section of the border between
       <a href="/wiki/Guinea" title="Guinea">
        Guinea
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Sierra_Leone" title="Sierra Leone">
        Sierra Leone
       </a>
       is defined by the parallel.
      </p>
      <p>
       The
       <a href="/wiki/Ten_Degree_Channel" title="Ten Degree Channel">
        Ten Degree Channel
       </a>
       in the
       <a href="/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">
        Indian Ocean
       </a>
       is named after the parallel.
      </p>
