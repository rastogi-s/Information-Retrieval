      <p>
       The
       <b>
        30th parallel south
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_latitude" title="Circle of latitude">
        circle of latitude
       </a>
       that is 30
       <a href="/wiki/Degree_(angle)" title="Degree (angle)">
        degrees
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/South" title="South">
        south
       </a>
       of the
       <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">
        Earth's
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">
        equatorial plane
       </a>
       . It crosses the
       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">
        Atlantic Ocean
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">
        Africa
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">
        Indian Ocean
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Australasia" title="Australasia">
        Australasia
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">
        Pacific Ocean
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">
        South America
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       At this latitude the sun is visible for 14 hours, 5 minutes during the
       <a href="/wiki/Summer_solstice" title="Summer solstice">
        December solstice
       </a>
       and 10 hours, 13 minutes during the
       <a href="/wiki/Winter_solstice" title="Winter solstice">
        June solstice
       </a>
       . On December 21, the sun is at 83.83 degrees up in the sky and at 36.17 degrees on June 21.
      </p>
