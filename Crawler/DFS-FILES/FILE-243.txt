      <p>
       The
       <b>
        meridian 80° west of
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prime_Meridian" title="Prime Meridian">
         Greenwich
        </a>
       </b>
       is a line of
       <a href="/wiki/Longitude" title="Longitude">
        longitude
       </a>
       that extends from the
       <a href="/wiki/North_Pole" title="North Pole">
        North Pole
       </a>
       across the
       <a href="/wiki/Arctic_Ocean" title="Arctic Ocean">
        Arctic Ocean
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">
        North America
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">
        Atlantic Ocean
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Caribbean_Sea" title="Caribbean Sea">
        Caribbean Sea
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Central_America" title="Central America">
        Central America
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">
        South America
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">
        Pacific Ocean
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Southern_Ocean" title="Southern Ocean">
        Southern Ocean
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="/wiki/Antarctica" title="Antarctica">
        Antarctica
       </a>
       to the
       <a href="/wiki/South_Pole" title="South Pole">
        South Pole
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       In
       <a href="/wiki/Antarctica" title="Antarctica">
        Antarctica
       </a>
       , the meridian defines the western limit of the
       <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">
        British
       </a>
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Antarctic_territorial_claims" title="List of Antarctic territorial claims">
        territorial claim
       </a>
       and passes through the
       <a href="/wiki/Chile" title="Chile">
        Chilean
       </a>
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Antarctic_territorial_claims" title="List of Antarctic territorial claims">
        claim
       </a>
       - the two claims overlap.
      </p>
      <p>
       The 80th meridian west forms a
       <a href="/wiki/Great_circle" title="Great circle">
        great circle
       </a>
       with the
       <a href="/wiki/100th_meridian_east" title="100th meridian east">
        100th meridian east
       </a>
       .
      </p>
