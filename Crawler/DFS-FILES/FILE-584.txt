      <p>
       <b>
        The Museum of the Sahrawi people's Liberation Army
       </b>
       is located in the
       <a href="/wiki/Sahrawi_refugee_camps" title="Sahrawi refugee camps">
        Sahrawi refugee camps
       </a>
       , in the southwest of
       <a href="/wiki/Algeria" title="Algeria">
        Algeria
       </a>
       . This museum is dedicated to the struggle for the independence of the
       <a href="/wiki/Western_Sahara" title="Western Sahara">
        Western Sahara
       </a>
       people.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LEFRIG-N12_1-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-LEFRIG-N12-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       It presents weapons, vehicles and uniforms used as well as abundant documentation history.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LEFRIG-N12_1-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-LEFRIG-N12-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       Currently it is also the seat of the Government of the
       <a href="/wiki/Sahrawi_Arab_Democratic_Republic" title="Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic">
        Sahrawi Arab Democratic Republic
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
      </p>
