      <p>
       The
       <b>
        meridian 20° east of
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prime_Meridian" title="Prime Meridian">
         Greenwich
        </a>
       </b>
       is a line of
       <a href="/wiki/Longitude" title="Longitude">
        longitude
       </a>
       that extends from the
       <a href="/wiki/North_Pole" title="North Pole">
        North Pole
       </a>
       across the
       <a href="/wiki/Arctic_Ocean" title="Arctic Ocean">
        Arctic Ocean
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">
        Europe
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">
        Africa
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">
        Atlantic
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">
        Indian
       </a>
       oceans, the
       <a href="/wiki/Southern_Ocean" title="Southern Ocean">
        Southern Ocean
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="/wiki/Antarctica" title="Antarctica">
        Antarctica
       </a>
       to the
       <a href="/wiki/South_Pole" title="South Pole">
        South Pole
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       The 20th meridian east forms a
       <a href="/wiki/Great_circle" title="Great circle">
        great circle
       </a>
       with the
       <a href="/wiki/160th_meridian_west" title="160th meridian west">
        160th meridian west
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Part of
       <a href="/wiki/Namibia" title="Namibia">
        Namibia
       </a>
       's borders with
       <a href="/wiki/Botswana" title="Botswana">
        Botswana
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/South_Africa" title="South Africa">
        South Africa
       </a>
       are defined by the meridian. The boundary between the
       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">
        Atlantic Ocean
       </a>
       and the
       <a href="/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">
        Indian Ocean
       </a>
       is defined by the meridian. The meridian defines the eastern limit of the
       <a href="/wiki/New_Swabia" title="New Swabia">
        New Swabia
       </a>
       area in
       <a href="/wiki/Queen_Maud_Land" title="Queen Maud Land">
        Queen Maud Land
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Antarctica" title="Antarctica">
        Antarctica
       </a>
       .
      </p>
