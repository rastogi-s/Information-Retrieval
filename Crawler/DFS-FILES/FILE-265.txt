      <p>
       The
       <b>
        70th parallel south
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_latitude" title="Circle of latitude">
        circle of latitude
       </a>
       that is 70
       <a href="/wiki/Degree_(angle)" title="Degree (angle)">
        degrees
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/South" title="South">
        south
       </a>
       of the
       <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">
        Earth's
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">
        equatorial plane
       </a>
       in the
       <a href="/wiki/Antarctic" title="Antarctic">
        Antarctic
       </a>
       . The parallel passes through the
       <a href="/wiki/Southern_Ocean" title="Southern Ocean">
        Southern Ocean
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Antarctica" title="Antarctica">
        Antarctica
       </a>
       .
      </p>
