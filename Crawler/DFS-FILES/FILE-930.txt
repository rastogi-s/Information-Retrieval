      <p>
       <b>
        Franc-Comtois
       </b>
       (
       <i>
        Frainc-Comtou
       </i>
       ), or
       <b>
        Jurassien
       </b>
       , is an
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/O%C3%AFl_language" title="Oïl language">
        Oïl language
       </a>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
        <a href="#cite_note-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
       spoken in the
       <a href="/wiki/Franche-Comt%C3%A9" title="Franche-Comté">
        Franche-Comté
       </a>
       region of France and in the
       <a href="/wiki/Canton_of_Jura" title="Canton of Jura">
        Canton of Jura
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Bernese_Jura" title="Bernese Jura">
        Bernese Jura
       </a>
       in Switzerland.
      </p>
