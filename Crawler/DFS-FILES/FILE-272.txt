      <p>
       The
       <b>
        45th parallel north
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Circle_of_latitude" title="Circle of latitude">
        circle of latitude
       </a>
       that is 45
       <a href="/wiki/Degree_(angle)" title="Degree (angle)">
        degrees
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/True_north" title="True north">
        north
       </a>
       of the
       <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">
        Earth's
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">
        equatorial plane
       </a>
       . It crosses
       <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">
        Europe
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">
        Asia
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">
        Pacific Ocean
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">
        North America
       </a>
       , and the
       <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Ocean" title="Atlantic Ocean">
        Atlantic Ocean
       </a>
       . The 45th parallel north is often called the halfway point between the
       <a href="/wiki/Equator" title="Equator">
        Equator
       </a>
       and the
       <a href="/wiki/North_Pole" title="North Pole">
        North Pole
       </a>
       , but the true halfway point is actually 16.2 kilometres (10.1 mi) north of the 45th parallel because the
       <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">
        Earth
       </a>
       is
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Oblate_spheroid" title="Oblate spheroid">
        oblate
       </a>
       ; that is, it bulges at the equator and is flattened at the poles.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       At this latitude the
       <a href="/wiki/Sun" title="Sun">
        sun
       </a>
       is visible for 15 hours, 37 minutes during the
       <a href="/wiki/Summer_solstice" title="Summer solstice">
        summer solstice
       </a>
       and 8 hours, 46 minutes during the
       <a href="/wiki/Winter_solstice" title="Winter solstice">
        winter solstice
       </a>
       . The midday sun stands 21.6 degrees above the southern horizon at the December solstice, 68.4 degrees at the June solstice, and exactly 45.0 degrees at the two equinoxes.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
        <a href="#cite_note-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>

      <p>
       In North America, the 45th parallel forms some boundaries, and passes through the U.S. states of
       <a href="/wiki/Oregon" title="Oregon">
        Oregon
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Idaho" title="Idaho">
        Idaho
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/South_Dakota" title="South Dakota">
        South Dakota
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Minnesota" title="Minnesota">
        Minnesota
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Wisconsin" title="Wisconsin">
        Wisconsin
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Michigan" title="Michigan">
        Michigan
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/New_York_(state)" title="New York (state)">
        New York
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Vermont" title="Vermont">
        Vermont
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/New_Hampshire" title="New Hampshire">
        New Hampshire
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Maine" title="Maine">
        Maine
       </a>
       and through the Canadian provinces of
       <a href="/wiki/Ontario" title="Ontario">
        Ontario
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">
        Quebec
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/New_Brunswick" title="New Brunswick">
        New Brunswick
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Nova_Scotia" title="Nova Scotia">
        Nova Scotia
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       All of mainland New Brunswick lies north of the 45th parallel. The southernmost point in mainland New Brunswick, just north of the 45th parallel, is Greens Point, approximately 90 kilometres (56 mi) west of
       <a href="/wiki/Saint_John,_New_Brunswick" title="Saint John, New Brunswick">
        Saint John
       </a>
       . Approximately two-thirds of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Deer_Island,_New_Brunswick" title="Deer Island, New Brunswick">
        Deer Island
       </a>
       , plus all of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Campobello_Island,_New_Brunswick" title="Campobello Island, New Brunswick">
        Campobello
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Grand_Manan" title="Grand Manan">
        Grand Manan
       </a>
       islands, are south of the 45th parallel.
      </p>
      <p>
       The parallel roughly bisects
       <a href="/wiki/Nova_Scotia_peninsula" title="Nova Scotia peninsula">
        mainland Nova Scotia
       </a>
       .
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Metropolitan_Halifax" title="Metropolitan Halifax">
        Halifax
       </a>
       is approximately 40 km (25 mi) south of the parallel.
      </p>
      <p>
       The 45th parallel roughly marks the
       <a href="/wiki/Canada%E2%80%93United_States_border" title="Canada–United States border">
        national border
       </a>
       between the
       <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">
        United States
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">
        Canada
       </a>
       between the
       <a href="/wiki/Saint_Lawrence_River" title="Saint Lawrence River">
        St. Lawrence
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Connecticut_River" title="Connecticut River">
        Connecticut
       </a>
       rivers (the northern borders of the
       <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">
        U.S. states
       </a>
       of
       <a href="/wiki/New_York_(state)" title="New York (state)">
        New York
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Vermont" title="Vermont">
        Vermont
       </a>
       with the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canadian_province" title="Canadian province">
        Canadian province
       </a>
       of
       <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">
        Quebec
       </a>
       ), where the parallel is sometimes called the "Canada line". The actual boundary of Vermont lies approximately 1 kilometre (3,300 ft) north of the parallel due to an error in the 1772 survey.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
        <a href="#cite_note-3">
         [3]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       In
       <a href="/wiki/Michigan" title="Michigan">
        Michigan
       </a>
       , the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Old_Mission_Peninsula" title="Old Mission Peninsula">
        Old Mission Peninsula
       </a>
       in
       <a href="/wiki/Grand_Traverse_Bay" title="Grand Traverse Bay">
        Grand Traverse Bay
       </a>
       ends just shy of the 45th parallel. Many guidebooks and signs at the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mission_Point_Lighthouse" title="Mission Point Lighthouse">
        Mission Point Lighthouse
       </a>
       describe it as being halfway between the equator and north pole. When the
       <a href="/wiki/Grand_Traverse_Bay" title="Grand Traverse Bay">
        Grand Traverse Bay
       </a>
       recedes below normal level, it is possible to walk out to the exact line.
      </p>
      <p>
       Farther west, the line roughly bisects the
       <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_area" title="Metropolitan area">
        metropolitan area
       </a>
       of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minneapolis-St._Paul" title="Minneapolis-St. Paul">
        Minneapolis-St. Paul
       </a>
       . In the
       <a href="/wiki/Western_United_States" title="Western United States">
        Western United States
       </a>
       , the parallel passes through the
       <a href="/wiki/Great_Plains" title="Great Plains">
        Great Plains
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Rocky_Mountains" title="Rocky Mountains">
        Rocky Mountains
       </a>
       , intersecting the
       <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Ocean" title="Pacific Ocean">
        Pacific
       </a>
       coast in
       <a href="/wiki/Oregon" title="Oregon">
        Oregon
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       The 45th parallel makes up most of the boundary between
       <a href="/wiki/Montana" title="Montana">
        Montana
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Wyoming" title="Wyoming">
        Wyoming
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Throughout the United States the parallel is marked in many places on highways by a sign proclaiming that the location is halfway between the North Pole and the equator.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4">
        <a href="#cite_note-4">
         [4]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Asia">
        Asia
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=45th_parallel_north&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Asia">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Continuing west, the parallel passes over vast stretches of the North Pacific Ocean before passing through
       <a href="/wiki/Rishiri-Rebun-Sarobetsu_National_Park" title="Rishiri-Rebun-Sarobetsu National Park">
        Rishiri-Rebun-Sarobetsu National Park
       </a>
       and the adjacent town of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Horonobe" title="Horonobe">
        Horonobe
       </a>
       on the northern tip of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hokkaid%C5%8D" title="Hokkaidō">
        Hokkaidō
       </a>
       , the northernmost of
       <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">
        Japan
       </a>
       's main islands. It continues through the northern part of the
       <a href="/wiki/Sea_of_Japan" title="Sea of Japan">
        Sea of Japan
       </a>
       , entering the Asian mainland on the coast of
       <a href="/wiki/Primorsky_Krai" title="Primorsky Krai">
        Primorsky Krai
       </a>
       in
       <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">
        Russia
       </a>
       , north of
       <a href="/wiki/Vladivostok" title="Vladivostok">
        Vladivostok
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       At
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Khanka_Lake" title="Khanka Lake">
        Khanka Lake
       </a>
       it enters
       <a href="/wiki/Northeast_China" title="Northeast China">
        northeast China
       </a>
       , cutting across
       <a href="/wiki/Heilongjiang" title="Heilongjiang">
        Heilongjiang
       </a>
       and continuing through part of
       <a href="/wiki/Jilin" title="Jilin">
        Jilin
       </a>
       and eastern
       <a href="/wiki/Inner_Mongolia" title="Inner Mongolia">
        Inner Mongolia
       </a>
       . Transecting southern
       <a href="/wiki/Mongolia" title="Mongolia">
        Mongolia
       </a>
       it passes through the provinces of
       <a href="/wiki/S%C3%BCkhbaatar_Province" title="Sükhbaatar Province">
        Sükhbaatar
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Dornogovi_Province" title="Dornogovi Province">
        Dornogovi
       </a>
       (and its capital
       <a href="/wiki/Sainshand" title="Sainshand">
        Sainshand
       </a>
       ),
       <a href="/wiki/Dundgovi_Province" title="Dundgovi Province">
        Dundgovi
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/%C3%96v%C3%B6rkhangai_Province" title="Övörkhangai Province">
        Övörkhangai
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Bayankhongor_Province" title="Bayankhongor Province">
        Bayankhongor
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Govi-Altai_Province" title="Govi-Altai Province">
        Govi-Altai
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="/wiki/Khovd_Province" title="Khovd Province">
        Khovd
       </a>
       . In
       <a href="/wiki/Northwest_China" title="Northwest China">
        northwest China
       </a>
       it passes through the
       <a href="/wiki/Ili_Kazakh_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture">
        Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture
       </a>
       in
       <a href="/wiki/Xinjiang" title="Xinjiang">
        Xinjiang
       </a>
       and the oil city of
       <a href="/wiki/Karamay" title="Karamay">
        Karamay
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       The parallel passes through southern
       <a href="/wiki/Kazakhstan" title="Kazakhstan">
        Kazakhstan
       </a>
       , intersecting the city of
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Burylbaytal&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Burylbaytal (page does not exist)">
        Burylbaytal
       </a>
       at the southern tip of
       <a href="/wiki/Lake_Balkhash" title="Lake Balkhash">
        Lake Balkhash
       </a>
       and the city of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Qyzylorda" title="Qyzylorda">
        Qyzylorda
       </a>
       further west. At the border with
       <a href="/wiki/Uzbekistan" title="Uzbekistan">
        Uzbekistan
       </a>
       it bisects the
       <a href="/wiki/Aral_Sea" title="Aral Sea">
        Aral Sea
       </a>
       and its toxic
       <a href="/wiki/Vozrozhdeniya_Island" title="Vozrozhdeniya Island">
        Vozrozhdeniya Island
       </a>
       peninsula, site of an abandoned Soviet
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bioweapons" title="Bioweapons">
        bioweapons
       </a>
       laboratory. It skirts the northern edge of the
       <a href="/wiki/Ustyurt_Plateau" title="Ustyurt Plateau">
        Ustyurt Plateau
       </a>
       , entering back into Kazakhstan before cutting across the northern tip of the
       <a href="/wiki/Caspian_Sea" title="Caspian Sea">
        Caspian Sea
       </a>
       and into
       <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">
        Europe
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">
        Russia
       </a>
       .
      </p>

      <p>
       In
       <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">
        Europe
       </a>
       the 45th parallel stretches between the Caspian Sea coast of the Russian
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Caucasus_(geographic_region)" title="Caucasus (geographic region)">
        Caucasus
       </a>
       in the east and
       <a href="/wiki/Bay_of_Biscay" title="Bay of Biscay">
        Bay of Biscay
       </a>
       coast of
       <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">
        France
       </a>
       in the west. In Russia it runs from the west coast of the Caspian Sea to the east coast of the
       <a href="/wiki/Black_Sea" title="Black Sea">
        Black Sea
       </a>
       , through the
       <a href="/wiki/Kalmykia" title="Kalmykia">
        Republic of Kalmykia
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Stavropol_Krai" title="Stavropol Krai">
        Stavropol Krai
       </a>
       and its capital
       <a href="/wiki/Stavropol" title="Stavropol">
        Stavropol
       </a>
       , and
       <a href="/wiki/Krasnodar_Krai" title="Krasnodar Krai">
        Krasnodar Krai
       </a>
       and its capital
       <a href="/wiki/Krasnodar" title="Krasnodar">
        Krasnodar
       </a>
       . In
       <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">
        Ukraine
       </a>
       it crosses the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crimean_Peninsula" title="Crimean Peninsula">
        Crimea
       </a>
       and its capital
       <a href="/wiki/Simferopol" title="Simferopol">
        Simferopol
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       Further west it passes through the
       <a href="/wiki/Balkans" title="Balkans">
        Balkans
       </a>
       :
       <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">
        Romania
       </a>
       (just north of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ploie%C5%9Fti" title="Ploieşti">
        Ploieşti
       </a>
       , and through
       <a href="/wiki/T%C3%A2rgu_Jiu" title="Târgu Jiu">
        Târgu Jiu
       </a>
       ), the
       <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">
        Serbian
       </a>
       autonomous province of
       <a href="/wiki/Vojvodina" title="Vojvodina">
        Vojvodina
       </a>
       , the eastern tip of
       <a href="/wiki/Croatia" title="Croatia">
        Croatia
       </a>
       , the northern edge of
       <a href="/wiki/Bosnia_and_Herzegovina" title="Bosnia and Herzegovina">
        Bosnia and Herzegovina
       </a>
       , and a section of
       <a href="/wiki/Adriatic_Sea" title="Adriatic Sea">
        Adriatic
       </a>
       Croatia. The capital city of
       <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">
        Serbia
       </a>
       –
       <a href="/wiki/Belgrade" title="Belgrade">
        Belgrade
       </a>
       is just south of the parallel.
      </p>
      <p>
       In northern
       <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">
        Italy
       </a>
       it parallels the river
       <a href="/wiki/Po_(river)" title="Po (river)">
        Po
       </a>
       , near
       <a href="/wiki/Rovigo" title="Rovigo">
        Rovigo
       </a>
       , passing by
       <a href="/wiki/Voghera" title="Voghera">
        Voghera
       </a>
       , then just south of
       <a href="/wiki/Turin" title="Turin">
        Turin
       </a>
       before passing into
       <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">
        France
       </a>
       in the
       <a href="/wiki/Cottian_Alps" title="Cottian Alps">
        Cottian Alps
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <p>
       In France, it crosses the river
       <a href="/wiki/Rh%C3%B4ne" title="Rhône">
        Rhône
       </a>
       at
       <a href="/wiki/Pont-de-l%27Is%C3%A8re" title="Pont-de-l'Isère">
        Pont-de-l'Isère
       </a>
       , just north of
       <a href="/wiki/Valence,_Dr%C3%B4me" title="Valence, Drôme">
        Valence, Drôme
       </a>
       and through
       <a href="/wiki/Grenoble" title="Grenoble">
        Grenoble
       </a>
       . It then continues across the
       <a href="/wiki/Massif_Central" title="Massif Central">
        Massif Central
       </a>
       and into the
       <a href="/wiki/Aquitaine" title="Aquitaine">
        Aquitaine
       </a>
       region. The city of
       <a href="/wiki/Bordeaux" title="Bordeaux">
        Bordeaux
       </a>
       is just south of the parallel.
      </p>
