      <p>
       The
       <b>
        Energia Lunar Expedition
       </b>
       was a project that was initiated by
       <a href="/wiki/Valentin_Glushko" title="Valentin Glushko">
        Valentin Glushko
       </a>
       during 1988 to create a
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lunar_base" title="Lunar base">
        lunar base
       </a>
       using the
       <a href="/wiki/Energia" title="Energia">
        Energia booster
       </a>
       . The moon base was to be eventually used for mining
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Helium_3" title="Helium 3">
        helium 3
       </a>
       from the lunar surface.
      </p>

      <p>
       The
       <a href="/wiki/Energia" title="Energia">
        Energia
       </a>
       expeditions involved at least two sections of hardware. which were placed into a
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Low_earth_orbit" title="Low earth orbit">
        Low earth orbit
       </a>
       in separate Energia launches.
