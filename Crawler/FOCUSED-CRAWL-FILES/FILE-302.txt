      <p>
       <b>
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saros_cycle" title="Saros cycle">
         Saros cycle
        </a>
        series 121 for
        <a href="/wiki/Lunar_eclipse" title="Lunar eclipse">
         lunar eclipses
        </a>
       </b>
       occurs at the moon's descending node. It began in 1029 and will end in 2526. It contains 84 member lunar eclipses, each separated by 18 years 11 and 1/3 days, and will last 1496.5 years.
      </p>
      <p>
       The series contains 29 total lunar eclipses between 1560 and 2021. It contains seven partial eclipses from 1398 to 1490 and seven more between 2039 and 2147.
      </p>
      <p>
       The last occurrence of this series was a total lunar eclipse on
       <a href="/wiki/May_2003_lunar_eclipse" title="May 2003 lunar eclipse">
        May 16, 2003
       </a>
       . The next occurrence on
       <a href="/wiki/May_2021_lunar_eclipse" title="May 2021 lunar eclipse">
        May 26, 2021
       </a>
       will be the last total lunar eclipse of this series.
      </p>
      <p>
       The longest totality occurred at series member 26, on October 18, 1660, lasting 102 minutes.
      </p>
