      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         Mr Moon
        </b>
       </i>
       is an
       <a href="/wiki/Animation" title="Animation">
        animated
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Children%27s_television_series" title="Children's television series">
        children's television series
       </a>
       produced by
       <a href="/wiki/Sparky_Animation" title="Sparky Animation">
        Sparky Animation
       </a>
       (Singapore), Skaramoosh (UK), and Title Entertainment (Canada) in 2009.
      </p>
      <p>
       It has been broadcast by
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Disney_Junior_(UK_%26_Ireland)" title="Disney Junior (UK &amp; Ireland)">
        Playhouse Disney
       </a>
       (UK and Ireland), TVO (Canada), ABC (Australia), NZTV (New Zealand), ATV (Turkey), Zee TV (India), KBS (South Korea), PBS (Thailand), and Space Toon (Indonesia).
      </p>
      <p>
       The series is of 52 episodes each of 12 minutes.
      </p>

      </p>

      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Premise_and_format">
        Premise and format
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">

      </h2>
      <p>
       Mr Moon is a children's television programme that teaches children the names of stars and constellations. The show focuses on singing, games, and activities which children can join along too.
