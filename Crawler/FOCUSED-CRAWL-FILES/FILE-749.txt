      <p>
       <b>
        Moon
       </b>
       is an
       <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">
        unincorporated community
       </a>
       located in the towns of
       <a href="/wiki/Bergen,_Marathon_County,_Wisconsin" title="Bergen, Marathon County, Wisconsin">
        Bergen
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Mosinee_(town),_Wisconsin" title="Mosinee (town), Wisconsin">
        Mosinee
       </a>
       , in
       <a href="/wiki/Marathon_County,_Wisconsin" title="Marathon County, Wisconsin">
        Marathon County
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Wisconsin" title="Wisconsin">
        Wisconsin
       </a>
       , United States. It is located at the intersction of County Road O and Moon Rd, approx 4/25 miles WSW of
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mosinee_(city),_Wisconsin" title="Mosinee (city), Wisconsin">
        Mosinee
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gnis_1-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-gnis-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
