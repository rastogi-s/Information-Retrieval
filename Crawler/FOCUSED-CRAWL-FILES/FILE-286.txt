      <p>
       <b>
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saros_cycle" title="Saros cycle">
         Saros cycle
        </a>
        series 105 for
        <a href="/wiki/Lunar_eclipse" title="Lunar eclipse">
         lunar eclipses
        </a>
       </b>
       occurs at the moon's descending node, repeating every 18 years 11 and 1/3 days. It contains 73 events between 566 August 16 and 1864 October 15.
