      <p>
       The
       <b>
        FreeX Moon
       </b>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">
        German
       </a>
       single-place,
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paraglider" title="Paraglider">
        paraglider
       </a>
       that was designed and produced by
       <a href="/wiki/FreeX" title="FreeX">
        FreeX
       </a>
       of
       <a href="/wiki/Egling" title="Egling">
        Egling
       </a>
       in the mid-2000s. It is now out of production.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WDLA04_1-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-WDLA04-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>

      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Design_and_development">
        Design and development
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=FreeX_Moon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Design and development">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       The Moon was designed as an intermediate glider. Like all FreeX wings it features internal diagonal bracing. The models are each named for their relative size.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WDLA04_1-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-WDLA04-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Variants">
        Variants
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=FreeX_Moon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Variants">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <dl>
       <dt>
        Moon S
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Small-sized model for lighter pilots. Its 11.1 m (36.4 ft) span wing has a wing area of 23.8 m
        <sup>
         2
        </sup>
        (256 sq ft), 49 cells and the
        <a href="/wiki/Aspect_ratio_(aeronautics)" title="Aspect ratio (aeronautics)">
         aspect ratio
        </a>
        is 5.2:1. The pilot weight range is 40 to 70 kg (88 to 154 lb). The glider model is DHV 1-2 GH certified.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WDLA04_1-2">
         <a href="#cite_note-WDLA04-1">
          [1]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </dd>
       <dt>
        Moon M
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Mid-sized model for medium-weight pilots. Its 11.8 m (38.7 ft) span wing has a wing area of 27.0 m
        <sup>
         2
        </sup>
        (291 sq ft), 49 cells and the aspect ratio is 5.2:1. The pilot weight range is 60 to 90 kg (132 to 198 lb). The glider model is DHV 1-2 GH certified.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WDLA04_1-3">
         <a href="#cite_note-WDLA04-1">
          [1]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </dd>
       <dt>
        Moon L
       </dt>
       <dd>
        Large-sized model for heavier pilots. Its 12.6 m (41.3 ft) span wing has a wing area of 30.4 m
        <sup>
         2
        </sup>
        (327 sq ft), 49 cells and the aspect ratio is 5.2:1. The pilot weight range is 80 to 110 kg (176 to 243 lb). The glider model is DHV 1-2 GH certified.
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WDLA04_1-4">
         <a href="#cite_note-WDLA04-1">
          [1]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </dd>
      </dl>
      <h2>
       <span id="Specifications_.28Moon_M.29">
       </span>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Specifications_(Moon_M)">
        Specifications (Moon M)
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=FreeX_Moon&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Specifications (Moon M)">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p style="font-size: 90%; margin:0;">
       <i>
        Data from
       </i>
       Bertrand
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WDLA04_1-5">
        <a href="#cite_note-WDLA04-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <p>
       <span style="font-size: 120%;">
        <b>
         General characteristics
        </b>
       </span>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Crew:
        </b>
        one
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Wingspan:
        </b>
        11.8 m (38 ft 9 in)
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Wing area:
        </b>
        27.0 m
        <sup>
         2
        </sup>
        (291 sq ft)
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Aspect ratio:
        </b>
        5.2:1
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       <span style="font-size: 120%;">
        <b>
         Performance
        </b>
       </span>
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         Maximum speed:
        </b>
        50 km/h (31 mph; 27 kn)
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Rate of sink:
        </b>
        1.1 m/s (220 ft/min)
       </li>
      </ul>
