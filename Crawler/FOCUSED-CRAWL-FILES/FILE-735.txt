      <p>
       "
       <b>
        Blue Moon
       </b>
       " is a song written,
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-allmusic_3-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-allmusic-3">
         [3]
        </a>
       </sup>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-discogs_2-3">
        <a href="#cite_note-discogs-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
       produced
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-discogs_2-4">
        <a href="#cite_note-discogs-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
       and performed by
       <a href="/wiki/Beck" title="Beck">
        Beck
       </a>
       , issued as the lead
       <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">
        single
       </a>
       from his twelfth studio album
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/Morning_Phase" title="Morning Phase">
         Morning Phase
        </a>
       </i>
       . The song is the musician's first release on
       <a href="/wiki/Capitol_Records" title="Capitol Records">
        Capitol Records
       </a>
       (after leaving his previous label,
       <a href="/wiki/Geffen_Records" title="Geffen Records">
        Geffen Records
       </a>
       ). The song was nominated for
       <a href="/wiki/Grammy_Award_for_Best_Rock_Song" title="Grammy Award for Best Rock Song">
        Best Rock Song
       </a>
       and
       <a href="/wiki/Grammy_Award_for_Best_Rock_Performance" title="Grammy Award for Best Rock Performance">
        Best Rock Performance
       </a>
       at the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/57th_Grammy_Awards" title="57th Grammy Awards">
        57th Grammy Awards
       </a>
       . Beck performed the song on
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/The_Ellen_DeGeneres_Show" title="The Ellen DeGeneres Show">
         Ellen
        </a>
       </i>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ellen_4-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-ellen-4">
         [4]
        </a>
       </sup>
       and
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/Saturday_Night_Live" title="Saturday Night Live">
         Saturday Night Live
        </a>
       </i>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-snl_5-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-snl-5">
         [5]
        </a>
       </sup>
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-snl2_6-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-snl2-6">
         [6]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>

      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Critical_reception">
        Critical reception
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Blue_Moon_(Beck_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Critical reception">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       The song has received positive reviews from critics. Kory Grow of
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/Rolling_Stone" title="Rolling Stone">
         Rolling Stone
        </a>
       </i>
       , while comparing the song's melody to material from Beck's 2002 album
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/Sea_Change_(album)" title="Sea Change (album)">
         Sea Change
        </a>
       </i>
       , called the song "anything but desolate", as well as "lush".
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-stone_7-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-stone-7">
         [7]
        </a>
       </sup>
       Leonie Cooper of
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/NME" title="NME">
         NME
        </a>
       </i>
       called the song "one of the most captivating things Beck has ever composed".
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nme_8-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-nme-8">
         [8]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Personnel">
        Personnel

        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
