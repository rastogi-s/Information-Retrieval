      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         The Moons of Jupiter
        </b>
       </i>
       is a book of
       <a href="/wiki/Short_story" title="Short story">
        short stories
       </a>
       by
       <a href="/wiki/Alice_Munro" title="Alice Munro">
        Alice Munro
       </a>
       , published by
       <a href="/wiki/Macmillan_of_Canada" title="Macmillan of Canada">
        Macmillan of Canada
       </a>
       in
       <a href="/wiki/1982_in_literature" title="1982 in literature">
        1982
       </a>
       . It was nominated for the
       <a href="/wiki/1982_Governor_General%27s_Awards" title="1982 Governor General's Awards">
        1982 Governor General's Award
       </a>
       for English Fiction. The title of the collection follows from the last short story in the collection "
       <a href="/wiki/The_Moons_of_Jupiter_(short_story)" title="The Moons of Jupiter (short story)">
        The Moons of Jupiter
       </a>
       ".
      </p>
