      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         Far Side of the Moon
        </b>
       </i>
       , in original
       <a href="/wiki/French_language" title="French language">
        French
       </a>
       ,
       <i>
        <b>
         La face cachée de la lune
        </b>
       </i>
       , is a 2003 film by
       <a href="/wiki/Robert_Lepage" title="Robert Lepage">
        Robert Lepage
       </a>
       . The film is based on the play by the same name by Lepage and Adam Nashman. The film is set in the context of the
       <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">
        USSR
       </a>
       -
       <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">
        United States
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Space_Race" title="Space Race">
        Space Race
       </a>
       of the 1960s. The leading characters are the brothers André and Philippe, both played by Robert Lepage. The film was
       <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">
        Canada's
       </a>
       official nomination for the
       <a href="/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Foreign_Language_Film" title="Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film">
        foreign-language Oscar
       </a>
       .
      </p>
