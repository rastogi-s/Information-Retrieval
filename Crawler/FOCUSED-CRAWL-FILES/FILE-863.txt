      <p>
       In the
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/UFO_(TV_series)" title="UFO (TV series)">
         UFO
        </a>
       </i>
       TV series, the
       <b>
        Lunar module
       </b>
       is the only SHADO
       <a href="/wiki/Spacecraft" title="Spacecraft">
        spacecraft
       </a>
       capable of Earth-Moon travel. On Earth, it can only take off and land docked with a
       <a href="/wiki/Mother_ship" title="Mother ship">
        mother ship
       </a>
       known as the Lunar Carrier, as part of its tail.
      </p>
      <p>
       This spacecraft takes off and lands on the Moon in an upright position on a launch pad in SHADO Moonbase.
      </p>
      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Description">
        Description
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Lunar_module_(UFO)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Description">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h2>
      <p>
       This spacecraft can't land in horizontal position, like the
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/Space:_1999" title="Space: 1999">
         Space: 1999
        </a>
       </i>
       <i>
        <a href="/wiki/List_of_Space:_1999_vehicles#Mark_IX_Hawk" title="List of Space: 1999 vehicles">
         Mark IX Hawk
        </a>
       </i>
       , but achieves
       <a href="/wiki/VTVL" title="VTVL">
        VTVL
       </a>
       operations at the SHADO Moonbase.
      </p>
