       <b>
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saros_cycle" title="Saros cycle">

        <a href="/wiki/Lunar_eclipse" title="Lunar eclipse">
         lunar eclipses
        </a>

      <h2>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Summary">
        Summary
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [

      </h2>
      <p>
       Lunar
       <a href="/wiki/Saros_(astronomy)" title="Saros (astronomy)">
        saros
       </a>
       series 128, repeating every 18 years and 11 days, has a total of 71 lunar eclipse events including 11 total lunar eclipses.
      </p>
