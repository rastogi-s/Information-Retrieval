      <p>
       This is a
       <b>
        list of retroreflectors on the Moon
       </b>
       , special devices left at five different sites on the
       <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">
        Moon
       </a>
       by the landing crews of the
       <a href="/wiki/Apollo_program" title="Apollo program">
        Apollo program
       </a>
       and by the remote landers of the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lunokhod_program" title="Lunokhod program">
        Lunokhod program
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-murphy_1-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-murphy-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       Lunar reflectors have enabled precise measurements of the
       <a href="/wiki/Lunar_distance_(astronomy)" title="Lunar distance (astronomy)">
        Earth–Moon distance
       </a>
       for four decades.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
        <a href="#cite_note-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
