      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         Moon
        </b>
       </i>
       is the ninth album by soprano saxophonist
       <a href="/wiki/Steve_Lacy" title="Steve Lacy">
        Steve Lacy
       </a>
       and was recorded in Rome in 1969 and originally released on the
       <a href="/wiki/BYG_Actuel" title="BYG Actuel">
        BYG Actuel
       </a>
       label. It features five compositions by Lacy performed by Lacy,
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Italo_Toni&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Italo Toni (page does not exist)">
        Italo Toni
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Claudo_Volonte&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Claudo Volonte (page does not exist)">
        Claudo Volonte
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Irene_Aebi" title="Irene Aebi">
        Irene Aebi
       </a>
       ,
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Marcello_Melis&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Marcello Melis (page does not exist)">
        Marcello Melis
       </a>
       and
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Jacques_Thollot&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Jacques Thollot (page does not exist)">
        Jacques Thollot
       </a>
       .
      </p>
