      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         Moon
        </b>
       </i>
       is the debut
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album">
        studio album
       </a>
       by American/British duo
       <a href="/wiki/Snowbird_(band)" title="Snowbird (band)">
        Snowbird
       </a>
       . It was released in January 2014 by
       <a href="/wiki/Bella_Union" title="Bella Union">
        Bella Union
       </a>
       . The album also contains a bonus second disc, titled
       <i>
        Luna
       </i>
       , consisting of remixes by RxGibbs.
      </p>
