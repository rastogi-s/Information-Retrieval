      <p>
       <b>
        <a href="/wiki/Saros_(astronomy)" title="Saros (astronomy)">
         Saros
        </a>
        129 for
        <a href="/wiki/Lunar_eclipse" title="Lunar eclipse">
         lunar eclipses
        </a>
       </b>
       occurs at the moon's descending node, repeats every 18 years 11 and 1/3 days. It contains 71 events.
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Solar_saros_136" title="Solar saros 136">
        Solar saros 136
       </a>
       interleaves with this lunar saros with an event occurring every 9 years 5 days alternating between each saros series.
