       <a href="/wiki/Echidna_(mythology)" title="Echidna (mythology)">
        Echidna
       </a>
       , the monstrous mate of Typhon. It orbits Typhon at ~1300 km, completing one orbit in about 11 days. Its diameter is estimated to be 89±6 km. Typhon is the first known binary
       <a href="/wiki/Centaur_(minor_planet)" title="Centaur (minor planet)">
        centaur
       </a>
       ,
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Noll2006-BinaryCentar_5-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-Noll2006-BinaryCentar-5">
         [5]
        </a>
       </sup>
       using an extended definition of a
       <a href="/wiki/Centaur_(minor_planet)" title="Centaur (minor planet)">
        centaur
       </a>
       as an object on a non-resonant (unstable) orbit with the perihelion inside the orbit of Neptune.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-DES_Elliot2005_6-0">
        <a href="#cite_note-DES_Elliot2005-6">
         [6]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>
