      <p>
       <b>
        Moon
       </b>
       is an
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unincorporated_community" title="Unincorporated community">
        unincorporated community
       </a>
       in
       <a href="/wiki/Morgan_County,_Kentucky" title="Morgan County, Kentucky">
        Morgan County, Kentucky
       </a>
       , United States. It lies along
       <a href="/wiki/Kentucky_Route_172" title="Kentucky Route 172">
        Kentucky Route 172
       </a>
       northeast of the city of
       <a href="/wiki/West_Liberty,_Kentucky" title="West Liberty, Kentucky">
        West Liberty, Kentucky
       </a>
       , the
       <a href="/wiki/County_seat" title="County seat">
        county seat
       </a>
       of Morgan County.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       Its elevation is 755 feet (230 m).
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2">
        <a href="#cite_note-2">
         [2]
        </a>
       </sup>
       The community's post office, with the
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ZIP_code" title="ZIP code">
        ZIP code
       </a>
       of 41457, closed in 1997.
