      <p>
       A
       <b>
        moonlet
       </b>
       ,
       <b>
        minor moon
       </b>
       ,
       <b>
        minor natural satellite
       </b>
       or
       <b>
        minor satellite
       </b>
       is a particularly small
       <a href="/wiki/Natural_satellite" title="Natural satellite">
        natural satellite
       </a>
       orbiting a
       <a href="/wiki/Planet" title="Planet">
        planet
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="/wiki/Dwarf_planet" title="Dwarf planet">
        dwarf planet
       </a>
       or other
       <a href="/wiki/Minor_planet" title="Minor planet">
        minor planet
       </a>
       . Three different types of small moons have been called moonlets:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        A belt of objects embedded in a planetary ring, as in
        <a href="/wiki/Rings_of_Saturn#A_Ring" title="Rings of Saturn">
         Saturn's A Ring
        </a>
        or
        <a href="/wiki/S/2009_S_1" title="S/2009 S 1">
         S/2009 S 1
        </a>
        in the
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/B_Ring" title="B Ring">
         B Ring
        </a>
        (
        <a href="/wiki/Moons_of_Saturn#Ring_moonlets" title="Moons of Saturn">
         "propeller" moonlets
        </a>
        )
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Tiscareno2006_1-0">
         <a href="#cite_note-Tiscareno2006-1">
          [1]
         </a>
        </sup>
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Sremcevic2007_2-0">
         <a href="#cite_note-Sremcevic2007-2">
          [2]
         </a>
        </sup>
        or in
        <a href="/wiki/Rings_of_Saturn#F_Ring" title="Rings of Saturn">
         Saturn's F Ring
        </a>
        .
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3">
         <a href="#cite_note-3">
          [3]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </li>
       <li>
        Occasionally
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asteroid_moon" title="Asteroid moon">
         asteroid moons
        </a>
        , such as the moons of
        <a href="/wiki/87_Sylvia" title="87 Sylvia">
         87 Sylvia
        </a>
        .
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Marchis05_4-0">
         <a href="#cite_note-Marchis05-4">
          [4]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </li>
       <li>
        <a href="/wiki/Subsatellite" title="Subsatellite">
         Subsatellites
        </a>
        .
        <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5">
         <a href="#cite_note-5">
          [5]
         </a>
        </sup>
       </li>
      </ul>
