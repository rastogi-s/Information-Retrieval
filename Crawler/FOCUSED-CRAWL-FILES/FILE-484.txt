      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         Strawberry Moon
        </b>
       </i>
       is a
       <a href="/wiki/Jazz" title="Jazz">
        jazz
       </a>
       album by
       <a href="/wiki/Grover_Washington_Jr." title="Grover Washington Jr.">
        Grover Washington Jr.
       </a>
       It was released in 1987 through
       <a href="/wiki/Columbia_Records" title="Columbia Records">
        Columbia Records
       </a>
       , catalog 40510. The album was self-produced by Grover Washington Jr, except for two tracks. The first of these, "Summer Nights," was produced by Marcus Miller and was released as a single, reaching #35 in the Billboard R&amp;B Charts. The second, "I Will Be Here for You," was co-produced by Washington and
       <a href="/wiki/Michael_J._Powell" title="Michael J. Powell">
        Michael J. Powell
       </a>
       .
      </p>
