      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         On the Moon
        </b>
       </i>
       is a 2004 album by
       <a href="/wiki/Jazz" title="Jazz">
        jazz
       </a>
       pianist, singer, and composer
       <a href="/wiki/Peter_Cincotti" title="Peter Cincotti">
        Peter Cincotti
       </a>
       . It was released on the
       <a href="/wiki/Concord_Records" title="Concord Records">
        Concord
       </a>
       label and produced by
       <a href="/wiki/Phil_Ramone" title="Phil Ramone">
        Phil Ramone
       </a>
       .
      </p>
