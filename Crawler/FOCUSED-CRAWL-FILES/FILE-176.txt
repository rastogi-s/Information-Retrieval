      <p>
       <b>
        Central lunar eclipses
       </b>
       are defined as
       <a href="/wiki/Lunar_eclipse" title="Lunar eclipse">
        lunar eclipses
       </a>
       in which part of the
       <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">
        Moon
       </a>
       passes through the
       <a href="/wiki/Antisolar_point" title="Antisolar point">
        center
       </a>
       of the
       <a href="/wiki/Earth%27s_shadow" title="Earth's shadow">
        Earth's shadow
       </a>
       .
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
       They are the darkest
       <a href="/wiki/Eclipse" title="Eclipse">
        eclipses
       </a>
       and are relatively rare.
      </p>
