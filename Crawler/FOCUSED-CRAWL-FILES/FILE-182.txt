      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Tritos_series">
        Tritos series
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=January_2000_lunar_eclipse&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Tritos series">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       The
       <a href="/wiki/Tritos" title="Tritos">
        tritos
       </a>
       series repeats 31 days short of 11 years at alternating nodes. Sequential events have incremental
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saros_cycle" title="Saros cycle">
        Saros cycle
       </a>
       indices.
      </p>
      <p>
       This series produces 20 total eclipses between April 24, 1967 and August 1, 2167, only being partial on November 19, 2021.
      </p>
