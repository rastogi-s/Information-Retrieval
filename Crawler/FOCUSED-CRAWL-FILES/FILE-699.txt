      <p>
       <i>
        <b>
         Three Moons Over Milford
        </b>
       </i>
       is an
       <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">
        American
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Science_fiction" title="Science fiction">
        science fiction
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Comedy-drama" title="Comedy-drama">
        dramedy
       </a>
       set in a picturesque small town in southern
       <a href="/wiki/Vermont" title="Vermont">
        Vermont
       </a>
       . Shortly before the series begins, an
       <a href="/wiki/Asteroid" title="Asteroid">
        asteroid
       </a>
       hits the
       <a href="/wiki/Moon" title="Moon">
        Moon
       </a>
       , shattering it into three fragments, threatening to eventually fall to
       <a href="/wiki/Earth" title="Earth">
        Earth
       </a>
       and end life. Thus, the continuous threat of an imminent "
       <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Judgment_Day" title="Judgment Day">
        Judgment Day
       </a>
       " causes many people to question their lives and live them to extremes by quitting jobs, indulging vices and living as if each day were their last.
      </p>
      <p>
       The show was cancelled because of low ratings in September 2006.
       <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1">
        <a href="#cite_note-1">
         [1]
        </a>
       </sup>
      </p>

      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Laura_Davis">
        Laura Davis
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Three_Moons_Over_Milford&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Laura Davis">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       Mother of the Davis family, who struggles with the emotional strain and major changes occurring in the wake of the moon incident. Her husband, Carl, a local industry leader, abandoned the family after the moon incident to live in a
       <a href="/wiki/Yurt" title="Yurt">
        yurt
       </a>
       some distance away from the family home, and has since left the country to climb the
       <a href="/wiki/Seven_Summits" title="Seven Summits">
        Seven Summits
       </a>
       . Shortly thereafter, funds are discovered missing from Carl's company, and the family's property and assets are seized, leaving the previously pampered Laura at loose ends to provide for her family. Laura is played by
       <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_McGovern" title="Elizabeth McGovern">
        Elizabeth McGovern
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h3>
       <span id="Albert_.22Mack.22_McIntyre">
       </span>
       <span class="mw-headline" id='Albert_"Mack"_McIntyre'>
        Albert "Mack" McIntyre
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Three_Moons_Over_Milford&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title='Edit section: Albert "Mack" McIntyre'>
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       A small town,
       <a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/down-to-Earth" title="wikt:down-to-Earth">
        down-to-earth
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Lawyer" title="Lawyer">
        lawyer
       </a>
       who helps the Davises in Lydia's
       <a href="/wiki/Arson" title="Arson">
        arson
       </a>
       case. A growing attraction develops between Mack and Laura Davis, which is nudged along when Mack's meddlesome mother hires Laura as Mack's secretary in order to drive a wedge between Mack and an old flame. Mack is played by
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Rob_Boltin&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Rob Boltin (page does not exist)">
        Rob Boltin
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Lydia_Davis">
        Lydia Davis
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Three_Moons_Over_Milford&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Lydia Davis">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       Teenage daughter of the Davis family. Her attempt to use a
       <a href="/wiki/Wicca" title="Wicca">
        Wicca
       </a>
       ceremony to save the Earth resulted in the accidental arson of the local high school. Even though she continues to defend the event as an accident, she is known as the "fire starter." She moves to a new school, where she is allowed nothing flammable and starts to fall in with a bad crowd. She is played by
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Teresa_Celentano&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Teresa Celentano (page does not exist)">
        Teresa Celentano
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Alex_Davis">
        Alex Davis
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Three_Moons_Over_Milford&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Alex Davis">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       Sixteen-year-old son of the Davis family, Alex is a
       <a href="/wiki/Mathematics" title="Mathematics">
        mathematics
       </a>
       <a href="/wiki/Child_prodigy" title="Child prodigy">
        prodigy
       </a>
       who struggles with vehicular issues. He becomes romantically involved with the 29-year-old Claire. He sold his brand new motorcycle to rent a house for him and his family right next door to Claire. He is played by
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Sam_Murphy&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Sam Murphy (page does not exist)">
        Sam Murphy
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Claire_Ling">
        Claire Ling
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Three_Moons_Over_Milford&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Claire Ling">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       Twenty-nine-year-old
       <a href="/wiki/Computer" title="Computer">
        computer
       </a>
       analyst who shares an attraction with the younger Alex Davis. She lives next door to Alex and the rest of the Davis family. Claire was unaware of Alex's true age at first, and thought he was an undergraduate taking time off from MIT. While still unsure of the relationship after discovering his real age, she finds herself still attracted to Alex. She is played by
       <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Samantha_Leigh_Quan&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Samantha Leigh Quan (page does not exist)">
        Samantha Leigh Quan
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h3>
       <span class="mw-headline" id="Michelle_Graybar">
        Michelle Graybar
       </span>
       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         [
        </span>
        <a href="/w/index.php?title=Three_Moons_Over_Milford&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Michelle Graybar">
         edit
        </a>
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
         ]
        </span>
       </span>
      </h3>
      <p>
       An opportunistic
       <a href="/wiki/Real_estate" title="Real estate">
        real estate
       </a>
       agent whose business-as-usual attitude flies in the face of the pending disaster. She is played by
       <a href="/wiki/Nora_Dunn" title="Nora Dunn">
        Nora Dunn
       </a>
       .
      </p>

       <span class="mw-headline" id="DVD_release">
        DVD release

       <span class="mw-editsection">
        <span class="mw-editsection-bracket">
