      <p>
       <b>
        <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saros_cycle" title="Saros cycle">
         Saros
        </a>
        series 132 for
        <a href="/wiki/Lunar_eclipse" title="Lunar eclipse">
         lunar eclipses
        </a>
       </b>
       occurs at the moon's ascending node, repeating 18 years 11 and 1/3 days. It contains 71 member events, with 12 total eclipses, starting in 2015 and ending in 2213.
      </p>
